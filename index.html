Introduction

The Principal and other school leaders promote and lead learning inspired by a belief in personal transformation and the flourishing of the whole person.

The school has high expectations for every student. Learning and teaching in this school is explicit and purposefully designed around the learning needs of each student. The Principal and other school leaders ensure all staff are informed by educational research, student data and curriculum evaluation processes to maximise their planning, pedagogy and assessment practices.

The school has structures to ensure that professional learning communities are high functioning teams that enhance teacher practice and performance, resulting in improved student learning. Staff members are engaged in a systematic approach to self-evaluation and improvement.

Access to quality feedback is a hallmark of the school. The Principal and other school leaders ensure that school structures maximize learning for all. Learning environments are calm but purposeful and interruptions to instructional time are kept to a minimum.
